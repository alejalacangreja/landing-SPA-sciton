Three wavelengths. One customized treatment.

HALO TRIBRID uses three independently controlled wavelengths in one treatment. This allows your provider to target different layers of concern instead of treating all skin issues the same way.

  1. 01 2940nm

    Texture resurfacing

    Helps improve the appearance of rough texture, surface irregularities and skin that no longer feels as smooth as it used to.

  2. 02 1927nm

    Pigment and tone

    Helps target visible discoloration, sun damage, dullness and uneven tone for brighter-looking skin.

  3. 03 1470nm

    Deeper skin renewal

    Supports deeper dermal renewal and overall skin quality, helping address concerns that are not only surface-level.

The goal is skin that looks smoother, brighter and more renewed.
